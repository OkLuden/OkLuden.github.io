projects come to life.

About Me

I am a 20 year old Computer Science student living in Cork, Ireland.
I was born in Poland, but moved at a young age. In my early years I discovered an interest in art.
I loved to create and watch my ideas come to life on paper! As I grew up I sprinkled
my creativity into everything I did. After highschool I decided I would like to pursue a career in technology
and share my creations digitally.

Now I am a hobby digital artist and computer science student.
I will continue to expand my horizon and discover new ways of sharing my creativity with the world!

Achievements

Winning UCC Hackathon 2022 My colleague and I teamed up to develop another game for the coding competition again. The game, "Wander", had an emphasis on visual aesthetics and design. We were very proud with the end result and second first place in a row!
Winning UCC Hackathon 2020 In a team, we developed a game for the college coding competition. We created an educational game "Monke Go Study". I was in charge of the visual design of the game, illustrating sprites/backgrounds/logos with PaintTool SAI.
Restaurant Logo 2019 A connection of mine opened a new restaurant "Indian Ocean" in Mallow, Ireland, and was looking for a logo to advertise it. Seeing my own design on a building was a very empowering experience.
Teen Graphic Novel 2018 The Teen Graphic Novel event allowed young artists to come together and create a comic book for Cork City Libraries. I was chosen to illustrate the cover for the 2018 edition of the comic. Working with various artists and hearing their ideas was a very interesting and fun experience.

About This Website

This website was fully developed by me.
HTML 5, CSS and JavaScript were used to develop this website, but I'd
like to expand my ability and implement a wider range of languages into my work.

After a few years of learning various coding languages and programming skills,
HTML and CSS particularly stood out to me. Being able to develop something
from scratch and watching it progress as you code made me fall
in love with the languages and I hope to do much more work with them in the future.
